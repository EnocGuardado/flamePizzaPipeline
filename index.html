About

Flame Pizzeria, a small restaurant with year-round twinkling lights and a 700-degree pizza oven that serves as the only heat source in the restaurant. The oven is used to make meatball sliders, puffy table bread, grilled lemon garlic chicken breast, thousand-layer potatoes, and of course, pizza. Neapolitan-style pies, paper-thin middle gives way to a thick outer crust with a nice char, chew and plenty of crisp bubbles.A curated toppings selection of caramelized red onions, oven-roasted mushrooms and red peppers, fennel sausage, fresh garlic, soppressata, olives, and more.
